Forest Path Birds

The forest path continues however in this area you can barely hear your own thoughts over the sounds of hundreds of tropical birds. There is an exit to the beach to your N , there is an unclimbable sheer cliff face to your E and to the S the trees become so closely entangled there is no way to get in.
