Rocky shore

you are standing on the west shore of this island, your ship sailing away behind you. There are several balane covered rocks beneath your feet wedged between two of them the map you were given, it probably fell there when you jumped off the boat. To your N the shore extends, however more sandy than rocky. To the S of you is another beach. Going inland, to the E, is a path leading into a forest.
